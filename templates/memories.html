The Journey Behind FunLife

What began as a curiosity about electronics has grown into a passion for designing intelligent systems and building software that solves real-world problems. From assembling Arduino circuits and programming embedded systems to developing web applications with Flask and Django, every project has strengthened my technical foundation. Along the way, I've explored Python, C++, MATLAB, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, Git, GitHub, and engineering simulation tools such as LTspice, Proteus, and GNU Radio. FunLife is more than a website it's a reflection of my continuous growth as an Electronics and Telecommunication Engineer, software developer, and lifelong learner who enjoys turning ideas into practical solutions.
